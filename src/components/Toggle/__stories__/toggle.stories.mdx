## Overview
Alllow users to turn an single option on or off. They are usually used to activate or deactivate a specific setting.

## Usag
<UsageGuidelines guidelines={[
  "Use toggles when your intent is to turn something on or off instantly.",
  "Let users know what happens when the toggle is switched by using a tooltip.",
  "Toggle can either be selected or not selected. They cannot be in an indeterminate state (unlike checkboxes).",
  "Use labels such as “on” and “off” or “enable” and “disable” to communicate the state of the toggle."
]}/>

<Tip title="Tip">
  If the user needs to choose an item from a set of options, use Radio button or Chechboxes instead.
</Tip>
