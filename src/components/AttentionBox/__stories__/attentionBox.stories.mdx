## Overview
Attention box lets users know important information within content areas, as close as possible to the content it’s about.

## Do’s and Don’ts
<ComponentRules
  rules={[
    {
      positive: {
        component:<AttentionBox title="Attention box title" text="Studies show that 100% of people who celebrate birthdays, will eventually die." componentClassName="box"/>,

      },
      negative: {
        component:<AttentionBox title="Attention box title" />,

      }
    },
    {

            <span className="monday-style-attention-box_text" >Catch up on updates from all your boards.</span>
            <AttentionBox text={<>Get your monday.com notifications directly on Slack<Button size={Button.sizes.SMALL}>Learn More</Button></>}  />
          </div>,
        description:"Use when you are speaking directly to a piece of content and the notification can be positioned close to the content."
      },
      negative: {
        component:
          <div>
            <span className="monday-style-attention-box_title">Inbox</span>
            <span className="monday-style-attention-box_text" >Catch up on updates from all your boards.</span>
            <AttentionBox text={<>7 days left on your free trial <Button size={Button.sizes.SMALL}>Learn More</Button></>} compact />
          </div>,
        description:<>Don’t use when a message applies at a system level, does not apply to a specific piece of content, or needs to alert all users. On those cases, use Alert banner.</>

    }
  ]}
